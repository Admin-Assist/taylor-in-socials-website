A calm process from first audit to approved content.

Built for busy venue owners who need content to feel organised, seasonal, and ready before offers, menus, events, or launch weekends arrive.

  1. AuditReview visibility, website clarity, offers, menus, events, reviews, and practical quick wins.
  2. PlanningChoose focused themes around quieter weekdays, seasonal offers, menus, events, and local customers.

  5. ReportingUse plain-English notes to see what is working and what should be promoted next.

Privacy Policy

Taylor In Socials collects the information you submit through website forms, booking requests, and client portal workflows so we can respond to enquiries, prepare audit recommendations, provide services, and manage client communication.

Information may include your name, email address, business name, website or social links, message details, client content notes, approval history, linked account status, and billing or retainer status. Sensitive passwords should not be submitted through this website.

We may use trusted third-party tools for hosting, analytics, booking, forms, email, payments, and secure account access. Data is kept only for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records.

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting taylorkinsella@taylorinsocials.com. This privacy information may be updated as Taylor In Socials grows.
